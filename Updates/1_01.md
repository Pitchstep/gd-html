# Update 1.01 - Level Saving!

Saving in the editor no longer overwrites your only level.

## What changed
- Added **My Levels**, a dedicated screen listing every level you've built and saved.
- The editor's Save button now prompts for a name and creates a new entry instead of silently overwriting your previous save.
- Each saved level can be played, re-opened for editing, published to the Community Hub, or deleted from the list.
- Fixed a bug where playing a level from the Community Hub quietly fell back to a built-in level instead of the one you actually clicked.

## Why it matters
You can now keep a whole library of your own levels around, iterate on them independently, and publish the ones you're happy with — without losing earlier work.
