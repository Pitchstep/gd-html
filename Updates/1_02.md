# Update 1.02 - Difficulties, Profiles & Search

A real difficulty system, plus two new ways to browse and check your stats.

## What changed
- Added difficulty tiers: Easy, Normal, Hard, Harder, Insane, and five ranks of Demon, each with its own star reward.
- The editor's Save dialog now asks for a difficulty, and that difficulty follows the level everywhere — My Levels, the Community Hub, and star payouts on completion.
- Added a **Profile** screen showing your orbs, stars, attempts, achievements, levels made, and (when signed in) your published level count.
- Added a **Search** screen for browsing community levels with **Recent**, **Top Liked**, and **Top Downloaded** tabs.
- Added a download counter that increments whenever someone plays a community level from its info page.

## Why it matters
Levels now carry a meaningful difficulty rating instead of a made-up star count, and there's finally a way to see who's making the most popular content.

Just reminder: These difficulties are subject to change.
